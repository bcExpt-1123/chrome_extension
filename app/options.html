FoxTel Click To Call Options

This extension provides one click connection between phone numbers on web pages and your softphone app, Skype or Hangouts application.
Protocol can be selected between tel, callto and sip depending on your external application support.
Extension recognizes US phone numbers by default. Users can opt to use custom regex pattern for detection.
